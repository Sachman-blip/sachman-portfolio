// 01 — STORY

I don't have a limit.

I'm Sachman, and I try to be the best at everything I touch — then I usually end up there. Engineering, sports, whatever I decide matters: I go all in until I'm above the rest. I've trained my mind to the point where I genuinely don't believe I have a ceiling. The only limit is the one you agree to, and I never signed up for one. Hand me a problem I've never seen and I'll take it apart, learn it cold, and outwork everyone in the room until it bends. Curiosity gets me in the door; refusing to settle is what keeps me ahead.

Go all in · Outwork everyone · No ceiling

Kickboxing was the first place I learned I don't have a limit unless I set one.
